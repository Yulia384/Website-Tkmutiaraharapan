About Me

Hello! I’m Yulia Permai br. Sihombing, M.Pd, the Principal of TK Mutiara Harapan under the Yayasan Pendidikan Karya Anak Bangsa Tanjung Medan.

With years of experience in early childhood and primary education, I’m passionate about creating joyful, character-based learning environments. My goal is to help children grow with creativity, faith, and kindness — preparing them to be confident learners and good citizens.

Education & Experience

  • Master of Education (M.Pd) – Universitas Negeri Medan
  • Head of TK Mutiara Harapan (2023 – Present)
  • Former Kindergarten and Primary School Teacher

Vision

To nurture students who are smart, creative, faithful, and compassionate, grounded in moral values and ready to face the modern world.

Personal Motto

“Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire.” – William Butler Yeats
